Mr. Pius Kwesi Sam (BSc. Biomedical Sciences, 2022)

Pius Kwasi Sam is a research assistant in the GGK lab with interests in molecular and cellular biology, bioinformatics, and computational drug discovery. His research interests specifically lies within understanding disease mechanisms and therapeutic inhibitory studies. He aims to uncover the molecular connections between neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) and non-communicable diseases such as cancer and neuropathologies, contributing to drug discovery and therapeutic development.
